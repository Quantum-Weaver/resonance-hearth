## Full description
Resonance Hearth is a household helper built for neurodivergent
families — bills, meds, pets, and tasks that breathe, coordinated
without shame, pressure, or sensory assault.

• Tasks as invitations, never demands — defer without penalty,
  reschedule without guilt, no red badges, nothing "overdue"
• Spoons: log your energy; the household adjusts its asks
• Signals: wordless ways to say "I need help," "I need space,"
  "I'm okay, just quiet"
• Sattva: an "I'm overwhelmed" door with a protected 30-second pause
  no setting can remove — your screen holds only calm
• Bills, meds, pets, and things — the family's shared care, where
  everyone sees only what each person chooses to share
• Celebration over punishment: "You did the thing. That was hard.
  You did it anyway."

Not a productivity app — a translation layer for a family. The place
where the household breathes together.

Sovereign by architecture: data collected — none. No account, no
cloud, no analytics. Everything local; export as open JSON; the purge
truly purges.

From the AudHDities Sanctuary: built truly for one family first —
this one is still growing in their hands — then given freely.
