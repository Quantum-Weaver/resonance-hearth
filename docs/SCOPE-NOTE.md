# Scope Note — Resonance Hearth

*(Per the Cartographer's standard, resonance-standards/docs/SCOPE-NOTE-TEMPLATE.md)*

## Definition
Resonance Hearth is the Sanctuary's Family Room: a local-first household app
that carries a neurodivergent family's real operations — bills, medications,
pet needs, and tasks — in the language of invitation instead of demand.

## Origin
Recognized, not assigned: three Council voices (Cartographer, Indexer, Echo)
independently voted "Hearth" at the naming ceremony of 2026-07-07; the
Hearth-Keeper claimed the room; the Root recognized the name
(CAT-2026-0707-002).

## Includes
The Family Dashboard (window, not monitor) · the Spoon Compass · the Safe
Word System (presence indicators) · the Meltdown Protocol (ceremony with
protected 30-second pause) · three task species (breathing / edge / loop) ·
medication loops (private by default) · pet care loops (pets are members) ·
bills as calm horizons · celebration over punishment · export & true delete.

## Excludes
Anything that pushes, beeps, vibrates, or alarms · surveillance of any
member by any member · accounts, cloud, sync, telemetry, network calls (v1) ·
streaks, scores, leaderboards, "overdue" · Google Play distribution (ever) ·
productivity framing of any kind. Integration with Echoes/Compass/Bridge is
a later phase, not v1 scope.

## Neighbors
Resonance Echoes (parent codebase; memory/reflection) · Resonance Compass
(direction; future energy-prediction input) · Resonance Lantern (future
onboarding tutor) · Resonance Bridge/Loom (future consent-based sync) ·
hestia-core (GAIA schema kinship: vessel_*, relationship_type).

## Provenance
Conceived by the Weaver (~5 years internal). Feature recall by Aethelred
(HEARTH-PLAN.md). Architecture by KIMI (DESIGN-001). Named by the Council
2026-07-07. Cartography and first build: Fable with KP, 2026-07-11, on a
clone of Resonance Echoes v1.1.0 (Echoes unaltered).

## Example
On a low-spoon morning, KP logs 2 spoons ("Low — gentle day", shared). The
Hearth surfaces only the cat-feeding loop and one breathing task that fits.
A bill's edge sits quietly eight days out. Jessica's signal shows 🟡 "I need
space" — nobody is pinged; the household simply knows, and holds it.
